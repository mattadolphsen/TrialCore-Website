Study operations

Workflows that keep the study moving.

PT

Participant Tracking

Track participant progress from consent through study completion, including enrollment, accrual, long-term follow-up, completion, and withdrawal.

VM

Visit Management

Manage protocol visits, visit windows, milestones, and study tasks from one organized schedule.

OV

Optional Visit Management

Add optional visits when needed without disrupting the required protocol schedule.

TM

Task Management

Keep operational work organized across teams, participants, visits, and studies.

Safety & compliance

Documentation that stays connected to the study record.

SR

Safety Reporting

Capture adverse events with severity grading, relatedness assessment, expectedness tracking, and regulatory reporting workflows.

PD

Protocol Deviations

Track deviations, corrective actions, follow-up, and visit-related exceptions with clear traceability.

CI

CTCAE Integration

Reference CTCAE terminology and severity guidance when documenting safety events.

AT

Audit Trails

Preserve an auditable history of changes and updates across participant and study records.

Oversight & administration

Visibility for the people keeping studies on track.

PV

Protocol Version Management

Maintain protocol version history as studies evolve over time.

RP

Reports

Review study activity, participant status, safety events, deviations, and operational trends.

RT

Recruitment Tracking

Track recruitment progress and participant movement from early interest through enrollment.

UP

User Roles & Permissions

Support appropriate access for administrators, supervisors, study staff, and auditors.
