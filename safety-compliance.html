Safety & Compliance

Built to support trustworthy study documentation.

TrialCore keeps safety information and compliance records organized so research teams can move with confidence, maintain traceability, and review documentation faster during audits, monitoring visits, or internal review cycles.

Compliance by design

Structured records without making the system feel heavy.

Track adverse events, protocol deviations, regulatory reporting, audit trails, and daily study operations in one unified, intuitive workspace.

Designed to support ALCOA+ principles, GCP expectations, and inspection-ready documentation.

AE

Adverse events

Capture event details, severity, relatedness, expectedness, outcome, and reporting status while keeping each event tied to the participant and study record.

DV

Protocol deviations

Track deviations, corrective actions, follow-up, and visit-related exceptions so nothing gets separated from the underlying study context.

AL

Audit trails

Track who changed what and when across participant and study records, with enough detail to support review and inspection.

CT

CTCAE support

Reference CTCAE terminology and severity guidance directly alongside the adverse event workflow.

PV

Protocol visit compliance

Follow required visits, windows, missed visits, and out-of-window exceptions so protocol compliance remains visible.

RR

Regulatory Reporting

Track PI, IRB, sponsor, FDA, and MedWatch reporting requirements and submission dates.

SAE

SAE Identification

Support serious adverse event review by connecting severity, seriousness criteria, hospitalization details, outcomes, and reporting history.

UP

UPIRTSO Identification

Flag events that may require additional review based on severity, unexpectedness, and relatedness.

AA

ALCOA+ Support

Support documentation that is attributable, legible, contemporaneous, original, accurate, complete, consistent, enduring, and available.
