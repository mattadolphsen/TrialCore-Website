About TrialCore

Built by clinical research professionals to simplify study operations.

TrialCore was created to address a familiar problem in clinical research: study management information is often spread across spreadsheets, shared drives, emails, regulatory binders, and disconnected systems. As studies become more complex, teams spend increasing amounts of time tracking operational work instead of focusing on participants, protocol execution, and data quality.

Company focus

  • Coordinator-informed product design
  • Participant-centered study operations
  • Protocol-driven visit and task tracking
  • Safety, deviation, and audit-ready documentation
Our mission

Make clinical research operations simpler, clearer, and more reliable.

TrialCore gives research teams a centralized operational workspace for participant management, protocol execution, safety reporting, deviations, documentation, and study oversight.

Why TrialCore exists

Many CTMS platforms are designed for large enterprise environments and can introduce unnecessary complexity for day-to-day study management. TrialCore was built to provide the structure research teams need without the visual or operational heaviness that slows coordinators down.

What it is meant to be

TrialCore is not intended to replace every research system. It is designed to become the operational hub that connects study teams to the information they need most: participant status, visit schedules, tasks, safety events, deviations, documents, reports, and audit trails.
